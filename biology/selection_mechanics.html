Selection Mechanics

Understanding Selective Agents vs. Selective Pressures

Key Concepts

Selective Agent

The "Who" or "What" — The Cause

The specific environmental factor that acts on the population. It is the literal thing doing the selecting.

  • A predator (e.g., hawk)
  • A drug (e.g., antibiotic)
  • A climate factor (e.g., drought)

Selective Pressure

The "Force" or "Effect" — The Consequence

The effect that the agent has on the population. It explains why survival is difficult.

  • Predation
  • Toxicity / Poisoning
  • Dehydration / Starvation
