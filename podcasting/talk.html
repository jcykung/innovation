The TALK Framework

Elevate Your Conversations: A Guide for Student Podcasters

“Be curious, not judgemental.”

— Walt Whitman

T

TOPICS

Navigate the Pyramid

The Topic Pyramid

Move beyond Small Talk to Tailored Talk & finally Deep Talk.

  • Preparation is key: Research your guest's world, recent work, and passions.
  • Dynamic Flow: Switch topics to keep energy high if things stall.
A

ASKING

Unleash Curiosity

Conversational Superheroes

Follow-up questions are the key to unlocking deep stories.

  • Ask MORE open-ended questions to invite elaboration.
  • Avoid "Boomerasking": Don't just ask questions to pivot back to talking about yourself.
L

LEVITY

Warmth & Connection

Build Rapport

Use humour and relaxed energy to create a comfortable space.

  • Be Authentic: Human connection fosters better stories than robotic professionalism.
  • Balance serious topics with relatable warmth.
K

KINDNESS

Practice Active Empathy

Psychological Safety

Be fully present. Listen without judgement.

  • Validate your guest's perspective and lived experiences.
  • Show genuine interest in their unique story. Stay centred on them.

Great Podcasts Are Built on Great Conversations. Start TALKing!
